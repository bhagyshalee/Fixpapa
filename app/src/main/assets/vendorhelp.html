1. Latest Job

  1. (a) How you can see the Latest job which is assign by “FIXPAPA” (admin) ?
    1. After clicking on Latest Job, All the latest jobs you can see on this page.

2. On Going Jobs

  1. (a) How can you see Open Calls?
    1. Click on the On “Going Jobs” Option can see left side on your panel.
    2. Now you can see all three options.
    3. Open: - In this option you can see all the open call which is not accepted by engineer.
    4. On Going: - In this option you can see all the Processing Jobs which is accepted by Engineer.
    5. Close: - in this option you can see all the completed Jobs.

3. How To Manage Engineers

  1. Click on the On “Manage Engineer” Option you can see left side on your panel.
  2. First you must need to add your Engineer by click on “Add New Engineer”.
  3. Now you can add all the information & Expertise of you Engineer.
  4. After that you can see detail of your all added Engineers.
  5. You can also edit, Block and change password of your engineer’s Profile.

4. My Info

  1. In this page you can see all the information which you was added when you create a vendor profile.
  2. You can also Edit some options like: - Owner Name, year of experience, Bank Details, Firm Name, Etc...

5. Account Setting

  1. With this option you can edit your Mail id and Mobile Number and you can also add your Alternative Number.

6. Manage Address

  1. With This option you can Edit or add a new Address of your firm.

7. Change Password

  1. With this Option you can change your Login Password.

8. Payment

  1. With This Option You can see your all the type of received Cash, Cheque, Online Payment Transaction and you can also check all your Monthly and yearly payment transection.
