1. Booking Repair Service

  1. A. How to book a Repair Service?
    1. Click on the Product which service you need.
    2. Follow the instructions.
    3. Brief your product Problem and Upload picture, follow the instructions.
    4. Once you have placed the booking successfully, wait for IT Professional to be assigned. A verified and trained “FIXPAPA” IT Professional will be assigned soon which you can also monitor from of your “web and app”.
    5. The assigned IT Professional will reach the address you have provided booking time and share the OTP to start service job.
    6. Once the job is completed by “FIXPAPA” IT Professional, request him to Generate the bill , once he complete the process you will get notification and Payment link to make the payment with different Payment Methods available.
    7. Once Payment is done you can review to “FIXPAPA” IT Professional and Payment receipt can also be viewed from the page.

2. Consult & buy

  1. B. How to Consult & buy a product?
    1. Click on the Product Which You Want to Buy.
    2. Follow the instructions & give complete Details.
    3. Once you have Post the Request for product wait for “FIXPAPA” IT Professional to call you, he/she will call and suggest you the best offer as per your requirement posted.
    4. If you agree to buy the product, “FIXPAPA” will deliver the product with in 48 hours on your desired location.
    5. Once You receive the product ask “FIXPAPA” IT Professional to Install the product and request him to Generate the bill , once he complete the process you will get notification and payment link to make the Payment with different Payment Methods available.
    6. Once Payment is done you can review to “FIXPAPA” IT Professional and Payment receipt can also be viewed from the page.

  1. (c) How to Get Bid for IT Setup?

    2. This Module of “FIXPAPA” is unique for the user (Home, Office, and Industry) who are looking or willing to setup a new IT Infra structure or Upgrade Existing IT Setup. With this a user Can get three individual bids from different vendors as per there requirement.
